Welcome_TO_ex.IGNORANTIA.COM

        

          
          
          
        

      

      

        

          

          
          

            
Ex Ignorantia is a Brazilian indie game studio creating narratives, mechanics & worlds that cross the veil of the impossible.

            
Our tight-knit team of weirdos and nerds builds on top of 6 years of TTRPG production, and carry under our editorial belt 2 physical corebooks and 50+ supplemental digital titles.

            
We’re lean, mean, slightly unhinged: let’s fckn go.

          

          
        


        

          

            

            
OURGAMES_Crow’sRequiem

          

          
          

            
Crow’s Requiem is a narrative survival game with elements of visual novel, RPG, comics, and puzzles.

            
Explore a Pandemicpunk world of moral dilemmas, manage your resources, watch the virus closely, and try to hold on to your humanity.

          

          
          

            
The_CROWS
You belong to a unique caste within the City: the Licensed Body Collectors. That unbinds you from the quarantine that isolates everyone else.

            
The_COLLECTION
Your training was brief, but you can handle the dead... right? Examine. Seal. Plug. Bag. By the time you finish, another dead body will already be waiting.

          

          
          

            
The_
PANDEMICPUNK
City
Explore New Horizon: temples inside forgotten malls, lawless industrial zones, and military bases of absolute control.

            
The_FACTIONS
Interact with the Military, Anarchist, and Cultist factions, who divide control over the city. Follow their orders with loyalty, or betray every fool who crosses your path.

OURGAMES_Last Roundat the End

          

          
          

            
Last Round at the End of the World is a folk-horror narrative puzzle game where you interpret divine messages, question characters, and choose who must die to keep an island alive.

            
Instead of fearing the sacrifice, like in The Wicker Man and Midsommar, this time, you are the Adjudicator.

          

          
          

            
The_RIDDLE
Once a year, the Spirit of the Waters speaks: it gives you a poetic riddle pointing to one soul among the villagers. Choose right, and one person drowns so the rest may live. Choose wrong, and the water takes everyone.

            
The_Interpretation
The Spirit’s words are never direct: a scar, an innocent gesture, an old sin. Every detail may be the key.

            
The_VILLAGERS
Speak with lovers, drunks, devotees, cowards, and survivors. They all know someone must die, but no one wants to be chosen.

            
The_RITUALS
Some truths must be earned: gamble at the pub, work in the cemetery, kill in the woods. Rituals and mini-games reveal clues ordinary conversations will not.

          

          
          

            
The_SACRIFICE
At the end of each year, say a name. Each death changes the village; every correct choice makes you more necessary. And perhaps more monstrous.
